Longmont Seeks Designs for Official City Flag
PUBLISHED: 2026-06-29T15:33:30.835202900+00:00 FORMAT: WATCH

The city of Longmont is inviting residents to submit designs for its official flag as part of an inclusive community engagement process Source.

Longmont has established a formal application process for interested individuals and organizations, ensuring that the design reflects the diverse culture and values of the city Source. The deadline for submissions is October 15, 2026. Interested parties can find detailed guidelines and submission forms on the City of Longmont’s official website.

The flag will serve as a symbol of unity and pride among Longmont residents, enhancing community cohesion and visibility Source. Community members are encouraged to participate by visiting the city's website or contacting the Public Information Office for more information. The selection process will involve input from a committee composed of local artists, historians, and civic leaders, ensuring that the chosen design resonates with Longmont’s identity.

While the exact criteria for evaluating submissions are yet to be finalized, the goal is to choose a design that captures the essence of the city's history, environment, and community spirit Source. The winner will receive recognition and a cash prize.
