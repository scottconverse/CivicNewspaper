Current Status of the Issue

The Building Services online permitting portal has been experiencing technical difficulties since June 24, 2026. This issue impacts a wide range of services including permits and inspections, which are essential for residential and commercial activities in Longmont. Residents and businesses have reported inconvenience due to the inability to submit or track permit applications online Source.

Impact on Daily Operations

The downtime affects various aspects of daily operations. Construction projects, which rely heavily on timely permits, are experiencing delays. Small business owners and property managers who depend on quick processing for renovations or expansions face significant disruptions. Moreover, the portal outage necessitates alternative methods for permit applications, such as in-person visits to the Building Services office Source.

Unclear Information

While the issue is known to be affecting the online permitting process, the specific technical cause of the downtime remains unclear. There is no detailed information provided regarding when the portal might resume full functionality or how long users can expect this inconvenience to continue. The city has not yet released an official statement on the status or timeline for resolution Source.
