Longmont Invites Applications for Official City Flag: The city of Longmont is seeking community input through an open application process to design its new official city flag, encouraging civic engagement and public participation in the creation of a symbol representing the city’s identity.

Headline: Longmont Launches Community-Driven Process to Design New Official City Flag

Nutgraf: The city of Longmont has initiated a public application process inviting community members to design its new official city flag, aiming to foster civic engagement and reflect the city’s identity through an inclusive design approach.

Process Overview: Longmont is currently seeking applications from community members interested in designing the city's new official flag. This initiative was announced on June 23, 2026 Source, encouraging residents to contribute their ideas and creative designs. The application process invites individuals or teams to submit proposals that will help shape Longmont’s future visual identity.

Community Involvement: The city is leveraging its Community Engagement Guide Source as a resource for those wishing to participate in the design process. This guide provides detailed steps and best practices on how community members can engage effectively, ensuring transparency and inclusivity throughout the design journey. Additionally, the Community Engagement Training Source is available for residents who wish to enhance their skills in public participation.

Next Steps and Uncertainties: The city has outlined a structured approach but remains open to community feedback at every stage. It remains unclear how the final design will be selected or what criteria will be used Source. Interested parties are encouraged to review the available resources, including the Community Engagement Guide and Training, before submitting their applications.

Specific Next Reporting Steps: The next reporting steps include:

  • Monitoring Application Submissions: Keep track of how many applications are received and from which sectors of the community.
  • Public Feedback Collection: Engage with residents to gather any additional insights or concerns regarding the flag design process.
  • Design Review Process: Follow the city’s review process for the selected designs, including public presentations and feedback sessions.

For more detailed information on how to participate, interested individuals can visit Longmont's official website or contact the city directly. Source
