New City Council Meeting Portal Aids Resident Access to Meetings

Published: 2026-06-29T16:39:54.337182+00:00 Format: watch

Longmont has introduced an enhanced Public Meeting Portal designed to streamline access to city council and advisory board meetings for residents. This tool is part of the city's ongoing effort to foster transparency and community engagement.

The new portal, Source, allows users to easily navigate through agendas by utilizing HTML bookmarks that link directly to video discussions on YouTube. Notably, this feature is currently available for City Council and Planning & Zoning Commission meetings only. Residents can find the agenda item of interest quickly without needing to search the entire video, significantly improving the user experience.

Additionally, the portal serves as a central repository for all meeting documents and packets, making it easier for residents to prepare for and follow along with discussions. Source notes that this initiative aims to make city governance more accessible and understandable for all Longmont residents.
