Longmont Seeks Applications for 2027 Human Service Agency Funding

Published: 2026-06-29T16:38:20.839071+00:00 Format: watch

The City of Longmont is currently accepting applications from human service agencies as part of its 2027 fiscal year funding process, aiming to support critical services provided by local organizations Source. This initiative reflects the city's commitment to enhancing community well-being and ensuring that essential services are available to residents.

Local non-profits and charitable organizations offering a range of human services such as mental health support, educational programs, and social assistance can apply for funding. The call for proposals is open to those providing vital aid to Longmont residents Source. Interested parties should review the detailed guidelines provided by the city government to ensure their applications meet the necessary criteria.

The application process will run through the end of October 2026, allowing ample time for eligible organizations to prepare and submit their proposals. Selected agencies will receive support that can significantly impact the lives of Longmont residents Source.
