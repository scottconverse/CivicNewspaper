Upcoming City Council Meetings Highlight Community Engagement and Governance
PUBLISHED: 2026-06-29T16:39:04.915282900+00:00 FORMAT: WATCH

The City of Longmont is scheduled to host several important meetings in July 2023, offering residents various opportunities to engage with local governance.

City Council Meetings

Regular sessions of the City Council will be held on Tuesday, July 7th, and Tuesday, July 14th. Both meetings are set for 7:00 pm to 11:00 pm at 350 Kimbark Street in Longmont. The agenda includes a pre-session on the evening of July 14th from 6:00 pm to 7:00 pm, which is intended for City staff briefings and planning sessions Source.

Open Forum

Residents are encouraged to participate in an Open Forum scheduled for Tuesday, July 7th. This forum provides a platform for public input on various city matters during the meeting Source.

Coffee with Council

For those interested in more informal engagement, there will be a "Coffee with Council" event on Saturday, July 18th, from 9:00 am to 10:00 am at 1200 Lashley Street Source.

Council at Longmont Farmers Market

Additionally, the City Council will be present at the Longmont Farmers Market on Saturday, July 25th, from 8:00 am to 10:00 am. This interaction aims to gather community feedback and promote local businesses Source.

Upcoming Events

Residents should also mark their calendars for the National Night Out event on Tuesday, August 4th, where the City Council will be present from 5:00 pm to 7:00 pm at 1251 Grandview Meadows Drive Source.

Note: Meetings prior to June 16, 2020, do not have specific video features for direct agenda item navigation. However, live video recordings and detailed agendas are available online for all recent meetings.
