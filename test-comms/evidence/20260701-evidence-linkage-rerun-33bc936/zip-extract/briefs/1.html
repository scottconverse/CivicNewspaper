Downtown Longmont's Public Library Votes on Rooftop Repair and Expansion Project

Published: 2026-07-01T07:39:20.713624500+00:00 Format: brief

Longmont residents will be taking an interest next Tuesday as council members prepare to vote for the $11,000-12,500 rooftop repair project at downtown's public library Source. This expansion includes not only repairs but also plans to increase capacity by 40%, which means patrons can expect larger reading and event spaces once completed. The total cost of this significant initiative is expected to be around $10 million over ten years. Residents should keep in mind that the scheduled vote next Tuesday will directly impact future library operations, with construction potentially leading to some disruptions during peak hours or specific events planned at other downtown venues Source.
