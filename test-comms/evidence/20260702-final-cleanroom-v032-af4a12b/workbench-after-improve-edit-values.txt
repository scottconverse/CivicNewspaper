Colorado General Assembly Wraps Up Session with Actionable Changes
--- edit Article Body (Markdown) ---
According to the information provided on May 27, 2026 [Source](evidence:19), The Colorado General Assembly concluded its session for the year and introduced several significant changes impacting our business community.

Key Actions Include:
- A billion-dollar budget shortfall proposal.
- New labor regulations discussions.
- Debates over energy policies.

The Longmont Area Chamber of Commerce played a pivotal role in advocating on behalf of businesses throughout these developments [Source](evidence:19).

For further details and updates, follow the progress at https://longmontchamber.org/
