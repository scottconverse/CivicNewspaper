City to Close Hover Street/CO 119 Intersection for Roof Work

Published: 2026-06-30T13:05:08.587273800+00:00 Format: watch

The city will temporarily shut down the intersection of Hover Street and CO 119 from June 28 due to roof work at the library. Residents and local drivers should plan alternative routes or expect delays during this time. This closure affects traffic patterns in the area, impacting both Hover Street and CO 119. The city advises drivers to seek detours to avoid the intersection during the scheduled hours of operation from June 28 onwards Source. The library will hold a contract vote on June 28, which is part of the ongoing project. While specific dates and times for the closure are not detailed in the current information, residents can expect changes to their usual travel routes. If you need to pass through this area or are planning your daily commute, it's advisable to check real-time traffic updates or follow local signage Source. Any potential safety concerns will be addressed by the city as part of the planning process. For further details on detour routes and additional information about the library project, residents can visit the city's official website or contact the transportation department directly Source.
