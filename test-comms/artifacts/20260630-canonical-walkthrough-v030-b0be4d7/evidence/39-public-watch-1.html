New Agenda Management Portal Features Video Bookmarks for City Council and Planning & Zoning Commission Meetings

Published: 2026-06-30T06:39:00.608794600+00:00 Format: watch

Longmont residents can now more easily access information during city council and planning and zoning commission meetings thanks to an updated agenda management portal. According to Source, the new feature bookmarks videos directly to specific items on the agenda, eliminating the need to search YouTube for relevant topics.

This improvement was implemented because HTML agendas with video bookmarks are now exclusively published for these two types of meetings. The change aims to enhance transparency and accessibility by making it simpler for citizens to follow discussions related to their interests Source.

While this development is valuable, the exact impact on public engagement remains unclear. Residents who regularly attend or watch city council and planning and zoning commission meetings may find the new feature particularly useful, but its broader effects are yet to be fully assessed.
