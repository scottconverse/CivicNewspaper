City Council Provides Guidance on Engaging in Lawmaking Processes

Published: 2026-06-30T11:16:18.435154300+00:00 Format: watch

Residents interested in shaping Longmont's laws are advised to first consult city staff or their local City Council member Source. The Council often directs staff to develop ordinances based on public feedback received during the Public Invited to be Heard segment of meetings. For further guidance, contact the City Clerk's Office at 303-651-8649. What to watch for next: Residents can stay informed by attending upcoming city council meetings and engaging in discussions during the Public Invited to be Heard segments.
