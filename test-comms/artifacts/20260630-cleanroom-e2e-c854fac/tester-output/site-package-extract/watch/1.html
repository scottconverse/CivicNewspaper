Longmont Museum Offers New Exhibits and Programs

Published: 2026-06-30T11:14:25.219931100+00:00 Format: watch

Longmont Museum has exciting new events and exhibits to explore this summer Source. Whether you're interested in history or looking for something fun with the family, there's plenty to see and do. The museum will feature a new exhibit on local natural history Source, showcasing the flora and fauna native to Longmont. Additionally, Discovery Days Source for early childhood visitors are scheduled throughout June and July. These interactive days offer hands-on activities and educational sessions designed to engage young minds. For those interested in attending auditorium programs, the museum has a variety of events lined up, including lectures, workshops, and special presentations Source. While the news provides brief updates on upcoming events and exhibits, more detailed information about specific dates and times will be announced soon. Museum-goers are encouraged to visit their website or contact the Longmont Museum directly for further details. These new offerings continue to enrich our community by providing cultural opportunities and educational experiences Source. The museum remains a vital resource for residents of all ages, offering insights into our local heritage and promoting lifelong learning.
